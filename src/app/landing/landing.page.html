Persian Flagship Learner’s Dictionary

The Persian Flagship Learner’s Dictionary has been developed through the generous support of the Language Flagship at the University of Maryland’s Persian Flagship Program under the supervision of Dr. Ali R. Abasi, with technical assistance and support from Dr. Aitor Arronte Alvarez of the Language Flagship Technology Innovation Center.


The Language Flagship is a public/private partnership sponsored by the National Security Education Program (NSEP) of the Department of Defense and administered by the Institute of International Education (IIE). The contents of this webapp do not necessarily reflect the position or policy of the government or IIE and no official Government or IIE endorsement should be inferred.


The Persian Flagship Learner’s Dictionary covers nearly all the vocabulary items that beginner and intermediate learners of Persian are likely to encounter across various commonly used textbooks currently available on the market. It is a practical learning tool, subject to continual revision and expansion. Questions and comments should be addressed to persian@umd.edu.


Many thanks to the University of Maryland students for their valuable suggestions, as well as to Ms. Elahe Amiri, Ms. Sayeh Solour, Ms. Bahar Jalehmahmoodi, and Ms. Manizhe Galledari for their important contributions to the project.
